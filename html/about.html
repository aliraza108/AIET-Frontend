AIET ( Alamgir Institute Of Engeering & Technology )

Alamgir Welfare Trust (AWT) works in all aspects of life whether it’s Health, Food, Education and many more. Now it’s time to expend Education aspect and cater all those who are eager to learn and improve themselves. For that Alamgir Welfare Trust has introduced Alamgir Institute of Engineering and Technology (AIET),it will provide quality technical education 100% free of change .

Believe of 8 year's Our Educational system

This project will not only help student to get technical skills but will also help to develop Student’s personality. Almost 5years back this idea was originated keeping in view the unexpected and uncontrolled increase in unemployment and lack of skilled labor. According to census in 2015-16 Pakistan’s literacy rate 58% that means 45% of people are still illiterate and many are below poverty line. This project of Alamgir Welfare Trust will not only cater underprivileged but also provide quality technical education to the youth belonging to middle class. It is dilemma of our society that we are believe education is only meant for male but it is a philosophy of Alamgir Welfare trust International which will be followed by AIET to serve the masses without discrimination of gender, caast,creed,color and religion Keeping in consideration the standards of corporate world this project of AWT id equipped with efficient labs, workshops, administration office and spacious library to inculcate the healthy habit of reading and provide student an exclusive environment to perform better in examinations and in their whole life. AIET will also provide a handsome monthly stipend to the they so that they could manage their expenses and get education which they would be unable to get due to financial issues. This might encourage students not to become a beggar but to become an independent and productive citizen of Pakistan. May Almighty Allah bless us in achieving our mission.
